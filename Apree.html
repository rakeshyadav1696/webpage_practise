How we help

Real Problems, Real Solutions

Walk through real scenarios that highlight how our solutions deliver personalized support that truly makes a difference.

Mark

Age: 52

Role: systems engineer

Health Status: New colorectal cancer diagnosis detected during routine colonoscopy, preparing to begin treatment. He also manages high blood pressure and stresses from balancing health, family and work.

Challenges: Mark struggles to coordinate multiple care teams, appointments, and the financial burden of managing cancer and chronic conditions. He worries about how treatment side effects will affect his finances, job, and mental well-being.

Meet Mark: A member who needs Chronic Condition Management for cance

Due to his high risk, Mark is assigned an RN Care Guide who reaches out via phone to help him navigate his care, book appointments, and educate on a prior authorization for new medication.

As part of his care plan, the RN Care Guide also sets up a health coaching connection visit, and an appointment with a behavioral health clinician.

The RN Guide connects with Mark through weekly phone calls to ensure his care needs are being met. They also guide him through the digital platform, showing him how to chat for support, review his benefits, and track his claims.
